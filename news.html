Culture and cross-culture study

Under the influence of novel coronavirus pneumonia, various measures and policies have been adopted by various countries to cope with the epidemic situation, and also reflect the culture of different countries.

CHINA

  • Chinese culture is rooted in Confucian culture, takes the "golden mean" as the basic code of conduct, and advocates that people should show love and kindness to others. Therefore, Chinese culture embodies the characteristics of group, which is not allowed to put personal value above group interests.
  • Chinese culture is collectivist, and the concept that collective interests are greater than individual interests is deeply rooted in Chinese society. In the epidemic, Chinese people are isolated at home, try their best not to go out, and minimize any form of assembly.

WESTERN COUNTRIES

Westerners are influenced by various ideologies and cultures, which makes a more relaxed, open and free environment has been formed. People pursue freedom and individuality.

Western countries advocate individualism. At the beginning of the epidemic, they still participated in all kinds of large and medium-sized gatherings, and the number of pedestrians on the street did not decrease or even wore masks.In Spain and Italy, although the government has taken "closure" measures, some people still often go out on the grounds of walking their dogs.
